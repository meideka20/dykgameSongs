Rules

  • This game is best enjoyed with an assistant.
  • Press play to begin hearing a random track from a video game. Once the track is playing, you have the option to pause the song or stop and restart it.
  • Choose the best option based on your familiarity with the song. You can change this at any time before you submit!
  • All of the games in the site will be present in the dropdown. If the game you're guessing isn't there, it's not a correct guess.
  • The title of the game is case sensitive; The title of the track is not.
  • The timer only counts up while the song is playing.
  • A note about the "close enough" button: Please use this sparingly and with great honor. Apply your best judgment as to whether or not you actually knew a song.
